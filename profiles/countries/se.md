# Country Profile — Sweden (SE)

**Language:** Swedish (sv) — or English for English-language campaigns
**Currency:** SEK (kr) or EUR for some transactions
**Regulatory body:** Livsmedelsverket (Swedish Food Agency) / EFSA

---

## Buyer Psychology

Swedes are highly educated, health-conscious, and deeply skeptical of marketing.
They have strong consumer protection culture and high trust in institutions — meaning
claims need to align with scientific consensus to be taken seriously.
Lagom (moderation, "just the right amount") is a core cultural value —
excess, hype, and aggressive sales tactics are culturally uncomfortable.
They adopt healthy habits sustainably, not through quick fixes.
Sustainability and natural/organic positioning is exceptionally important.

**Core emotional drivers:**
- Long-term wellbeing and health maintenance
- Evidence-based decisions
- Sustainability and natural credentials
- Fairness and transparency
- Balanced, moderate approach to health (not "fix everything overnight")

---

## Tone & Voice

- **Calm, balanced, and informed.** No excitement, no urgency.
- **Science-backed.** Mechanism must precede benefit claim.
- **Sustainable framing.** "Long-term health support" > "fast results"
- **Transparent.** Acknowledge what the product can and cannot do.
- Swedish copy: informal "du" is standard

---

## Headline Formulas (SE)

```
[Hur MEKANISM stödjer MÅLGRUPP efter 40]
[Den vetenskapliga förklaringen till PROBLEM — och vad som faktiskt hjälper]
[Naturliga ingredienser för RESULTAT — en balanserad approach]
```

---

## Trust Triggers (SE)

- "Kliniskt studerade ingredienser" (clinically studied)
- "Naturliga ingredienser utan tillsatser" (natural, no additives)
- "GMP-certifierad tillverkning"
- "Hållbar formel" (sustainable formula)
- "60 dagars nöjdhetsgaranti"
- "Fri frakt till Sverige"
- EFSA-compliant health claims
- Real Swedish customer names and cities (Stockholm, Göteborg, Malmö)

---

## Discount & Pricing Psychology (SE)

- Use SEK (kr) — always
- Swedes prefer quality framing over discount: "Premiumkvalitet till ett rimligt pris"
- Long-term value: "3-månaders kur — det rekommenderade upplägget"
- "Fri frakt" (free shipping) works
- Fake urgency: completely ineffective and damaging to credibility
