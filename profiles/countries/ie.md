# Country Profile — Ireland (IE)

**Language:** English (en-IE) — British spellings, warm Irish tone
**Currency:** EUR (€)
**Regulatory body:** HPRA (Health Products Regulatory Authority) / EFSA

---

## Buyer Psychology

Irish consumers share much of the UK's skepticism toward hype, but are notably warmer
and more community-oriented. They value storytelling and human connection in copy.
They are health-conscious but approach supplements with healthy skepticism — a genuine
story and strong guarantee convert well.
Ireland has strong diaspora connections with the US — some American directness is accepted,
more than in the UK.

**Core emotional drivers:**
- Community and family wellbeing
- Authenticity — genuine stories over polished marketing
- Value for money (Irish consumers are price-conscious)
- "Worth giving a go" — moderate optimism with risk removal (guarantee matters)
- Natural/clean ingredients resonate

---

## Tone & Voice

- **Warm and conversational.** More personal than UK/AU copy.
- **Honest.** Irish buyers detect and reject "salesy" language quickly.
- **Storytelling.** Open with a human situation, not a bold claim.
- British spellings throughout (colour, favour, recognise)
- Moderate urgency is acceptable


---

## Trust Triggers (IE)

- "Natural ingredients"
- "Made in an FDA-registered, GMP-certified facility"
- "Ships to Ireland" / "Free delivery to Ireland"
- "60-day money-back guarantee — no hassle"
- Real Irish customer names and locations (Dublin, Cork, Galway, Limerick)
- HPRA/EFSA compliant language

---

## Discount & Pricing Psychology (IE)

- Always use € (Euro)
- "Save €X" framing
- "Free shipping to Ireland" is a strong trigger
- Multi-bottle value framing: "3-month supply — the full course"

---

## Legal / Footer (IE)

EFSA/HPRA disclaimer:
> "These statements have not been evaluated by the Health Products Regulatory Authority (HPRA).
> [Product] is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."

GDPR applies fully — same requirements as DE but slightly less strictly enforced.

Distributor notice: "independent authorised distributor"
