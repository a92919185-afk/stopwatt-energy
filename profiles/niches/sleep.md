# Niche Profile — Sleep / Relaxation

**Category:** Sleep support, relaxation, stress and anxiety supplements
**Target audience:** Adults 30–65, poor sleep quality, racing mind, fatigue from insomnia
**Primary fear:** Chronic exhaustion affecting health, work, relationships

---

## The Core Problem Narrative

1. **The cause:** Melatonin production declines with age. Cortisol dysregulation from chronic
   stress disrupts the sleep-wake cycle. GABA activity decreases. Screen blue light
   suppresses natural melatonin release.

2. **The symptoms:** Difficulty falling asleep, waking at 2-3am, unrefreshing sleep,
   morning fatigue, racing thoughts at night, dependence on sleep medication.

3. **The emotional consequences:** Exhaustion affecting work performance, irritability,
   relationship tension, health fears (immune system, cognitive decline from poor sleep).

4. **The mechanism:** Adaptogens calm the HPA axis (cortisol). GABA precursors calm
   neural activity. Melatonin precursors (5-HTP, L-Tryptophan) restore sleep onset.
