# Niche Profile — Weight Loss / Metabolism

**Category:** Fat burners, metabolism support, appetite control supplements
**Target audience:** Adults 30–65, stubborn weight gain, failed diets, metabolic slowdown
**Primary fear:** Never losing the weight, health consequences, low self-confidence

---

## The Core Problem Narrative

1. **The cause:** After 30-35, basal metabolic rate declines ~2-3% per decade. Insulin sensitivity
   decreases. Cortisol from chronic stress promotes fat storage (especially visceral/belly fat).
   Leptin resistance develops, blunting the "I'm full" signal.

2. **The symptoms:** Weight gain despite same diet, stubborn belly fat, constant cravings,
   energy crashes, inability to lose weight even when "eating well".

3. **The emotional consequences:** Clothes don't fit, avoiding mirrors, low energy,
   embarrassment, health fears (diabetes, heart disease), past diets failed.

4. **The mechanism:** Thermogenic ingredients boost metabolic rate. Appetite regulators
   restore leptin sensitivity. Blood sugar stabilizers reduce cravings and fat storage signals.

### Features section — ingredient framing
- Green Tea Extract (EGCG): activates AMPK enzyme → increases fat oxidation
- Berberine: improves insulin sensitivity → reduces fat storage signals
- Glucomannan: expands in stomach → appetite suppression for 4-6 hours
- CLA (Conjugated Linoleic Acid): shifts body composition → fat loss, muscle retention
- Chromium: regulates blood sugar → reduces cravings
- L-Carnitine: transports fatty acids into mitochondria → fat burned for energy

### Testimonials — outcomes to emphasize

- Cravings dramatically reduced
- Clothes fit again
- More energy (not just weight loss)
- Belly fat specifically reduced
- "I wasn't hungry between meals for the first time in years"

### FAQ — common objections
- "I've tried everything — why would this work?" → explain the metabolic mechanism, not just "our formula is better"
- "Is this a stimulant?" → depends on formula — address directly
- "Do I need to diet?" → works best with a balanced diet, but addresses the metabolic root cause
- "How long until I see results?" → reduced cravings: 1-2 weeks; visible weight change: 4-8 weeks

## ⚠️ Copy Restrictions (Weight Loss — Very Strictly Regulated)

❌ Never state specific weight loss amounts: "Lose 30 lbs in 30 days"
❌ Never use before/after images without FTC-compliant disclaimers
❌ "Guaranteed weight loss" — illegal claim
❌ "Burns fat while you sleep" — requires clinical substantiation
✅ Use: "supports healthy metabolism", "helps manage appetite", "promotes fat oxidation"
✅ Use: "as part of a healthy diet and exercise program" disclaimer
