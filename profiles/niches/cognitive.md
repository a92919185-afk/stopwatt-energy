# Niche Profile — Cognitive / Brain Health / Focus

**Category:** Nootropics, memory support, focus, brain health supplements
**Target audience:** Adults 35–70, brain fog, memory lapses, poor focus, cognitive aging fear
**Primary fear:** Dementia/Alzheimer's fear, losing mental sharpness, professional decline

---

## The Core Problem Narrative

1. **The cause:** After 40, cerebral blood flow decreases. Neuroinflammation increases.
   Acetylcholine production declines (memory neurotransmitter). Mitochondrial energy
   in neurons decreases. Oxidative stress damages myelin sheaths.

2. **The symptoms:** Brain fog, forgetting names/words, difficulty concentrating, mental
   fatigue, slower thinking, losing train of thought mid-sentence.

3. **The emotional consequences:** Fear of early cognitive decline/dementia,
   performance anxiety at work, embarrassment in social situations, feeling "less sharp".

4. **The mechanism:** Nootropics increase cerebral blood flow (Ginkgo Biloba, Vinpocetine),
   boost acetylcholine (Alpha-GPC, Huperzine A), provide neuronal energy (Lion's Mane,
   CoQ10), and reduce neuroinflammation (Phosphatidylserine, Bacopa).
