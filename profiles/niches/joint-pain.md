# Niche Profile — Joint Pain / Mobility

**Category:** Joint support, anti-inflammatory, mobility and flexibility supplements
**Target audience:** Adults 45–75, knee/hip/back pain, arthritis, reduced mobility
**Primary fear:** Losing independence, inability to do activities they love, worsening pain

---

## The Core Problem Narrative

1. **The cause:** Cartilage lacks blood vessels — it regenerates slowly and degrades with age.
   Collagen type II production decreases after 30. Chronic low-grade inflammation (from diet,
   oxidative stress, excess weight) accelerates joint degradation.

2. **The symptoms:** Morning stiffness, pain with movement, reduced range of motion,
   aching knees/hips/hands, difficulty with stairs, swelling after activity.

3. **The emotional consequences:** Can't play with grandchildren, gave up walking/hiking/golf,
   pain medication dependence, fear of being a "burden", losing freedom.

4. **The mechanism:** Collagen peptides provide building blocks for cartilage repair.
   Glucosamine/Chondroitin support cartilage matrix. Anti-inflammatory ingredients
   (Turmeric/Curcumin, Boswellia) reduce the inflammatory cascade that destroys joint tissue.

### Features — ingredient framing
- Collagen Type II: direct cartilage building block → structural joint support
- Glucosamine Sulfate: stimulates cartilage synthesis → long-term structural repair
- Chondroitin Sulfate: retains water in cartilage → cushioning and shock absorption
- Turmeric/Curcumin (with BioPerine): inhibits COX-2 inflammatory enzymes → reduced joint inflammation
- Boswellia Serrata: blocks 5-LOX enzyme → reduces arthritis-related inflammation
- MSM (Methylsulfonylmethane): sulfur for connective tissue formation → reduced stiffness
- Hyaluronic Acid: joint lubrication → smoother movement
