# Niche Profile — Male Vitality / Testosterone Support

**Category:** Men's health, testosterone support, energy and libido supplements
**Target audience:** Men 38–65, declining energy, reduced drive, performance concerns
**Primary fear:** Losing masculinity, irrelevance, disappointing partner, "getting old"

---

## The Core Problem Narrative

1. **The cause:** After 35-40, total testosterone declines ~1-2% per year. More importantly,
   Sex Hormone Binding Globulin (SHBG) increases with age, binding free testosterone and
   making it unavailable. Chronic stress elevates cortisol, further suppressing testosterone.

2. **The symptoms:** Low energy, poor gym performance, reduced libido, difficulty sleeping,
   brain fog, increased body fat (especially belly), loss of motivation and drive.

3. **The emotional consequences:** Feeling invisible, disappointing partner, losing competitive
   edge at work, feeling like a shadow of who you were at 30.

4. **The mechanism:** Certain plant compounds (Tongkat Ali, Nettle Root, Boron) release
   testosterone from SHBG binding. Others (Horny Goat Weed) improve nitric oxide and
   blood flow. Adaptogens (Sarsaparilla, Ashwagandha) reduce cortisol.
