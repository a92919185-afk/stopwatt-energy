# Niche Profile — Vision / Eye Health

**Category:** Eye health dietary supplements
**Target audience:** Adults 45–75, deteriorating vision, reading difficulty, night driving issues
**Primary fear:** Losing independence, missing life's details, dependency on glasses

---

## The Core Problem Narrative

The biological story (use this as the foundation of copy):

1. **The cause:** After 40, macular pigment optical density (MPOD) declines. Oxidative stress from
   blue light, UV exposure, and free radicals damages photoreceptor cells. Nutrient deficiencies
   (especially Lutein, Zeaxanthin, Omega-3 DHA) accelerate this.

2. **The symptoms:** Blurry vision, difficulty reading small print, halos around lights at night,
   eye strain after screens, reduced contrast sensitivity, slow dark adaptation.

3. **The emotional consequences:** Can't read menus, struggling with night driving, missing your
   grandchildren's faces, dependence on stronger and stronger glasses.

4. **The mechanism:** Protective macular pigment can be replenished through the right nutrients.
   Lutein and Zeaxanthin filter blue light and neutralize free radicals at the macula.
   Omega-3 DHA rebuilds photoreceptor cell membranes.
