## PART 5 — COPY DIRECTION PER SECTION

### ALERT BAR
**Direction:** Social proof + Guarantee. German market values guarantees and certifications.
**Avoid:** Over-hyped claims. German consumers are skeptical of marketing language.

### HERO HEADLINE
**Direction:** Focus on natural GLP-1 support. Use "natürliche" prominently. Loss aversion: "Endlich Stoffwechsel-Energie ohne Stimulanzien."
**Avoid:** Medical terminology that feels like a drug. "Booster" is acceptable in German.

### HERO SUBTEXT
**Direction:** Subtext 1 — acknowledge the metabolic struggle. Subtext 2 — differentiate with InnoSlim and natural ingredients.
**Must have:** "Unterstützt metabolische Balance, Energie & Verdauung"

### BENEFITS SECTION
**Direction:** P1 — Name the root cause: metabolic imbalance after 40. P2 — Emotional consequence: feeling sluggish affects quality of life. P3 — Bridge to InnoSlim and natural GLP-1 support.
**The "yes, exactly" moment:** "Ich fühle mich einfach energielos und mein Stoffwechsel ist langsamer." → Mirror in P2.

### FEATURES
**Direction:** Lead with InnoSlim (the unique proprietary blend). Follow with Grüntee-Extrakt (EGCG 50%), Astragalus, Cholin. Each must answer "which means" specific to German daily life.
**Lead ingredient:** InnoSlim — sounds premium, clinically backed.

### SCIENCE
**Direction:** 3-step mechanism: 1) Natural GLP-1 receptor support, 2) Metabolic activation, 3) Energy optimization. Language: accessible but credible. Avoid overly clinical German.
**Language level:** More accessible. Use "funktioniert" over "funktioniert physiologisch".

### REVIEWS
**Direction:** Mirror profiles: 40-something German women who felt sluggish and found renewed energy. Specific outcome word: "Energie" "Verdauung" "Wohlbefinden"
**Must-have outcome:** "Ich habe endlich wieder Energie für den Alltag."

### FAQ
**Direction:** Top 3 objections: Does it work? Is it safe? What if it doesn't work? Use the exact copy from the official page FAQ but adapt for our format.
**Critical Q:** "Funktioniert das überhaupt?" — must be answered with ingredient transparency + lab testing + guarantee

### CTA
**Direction:** "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now). Guarantee: "60 Tage Geld-zurück-Garantie — ohne Wenn und Aber"
**Urgency:** Limited-time framing if real. Otherwise avoid fake scarcity.
